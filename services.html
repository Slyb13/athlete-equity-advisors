What We Offer

Our
Services

Comprehensive support for student-athletes navigating NIL, the transfer portal, brand building, and long-term financial structure.

01

NIL Consultation

The NIL era has transformed college athletics, creating new opportunities and new challenges for student-athletes. We help athletes understand their market value, evaluate endorsement opportunities, and navigate NIL agreements with confidence and full compliance.

Our team reviews every deal, explains eligibility considerations, and ensures every opportunity aligns with the athlete's goals and long-term development — not just the short-term payday.

  • Deal review and contract negotiation
  • Market value assessment and pricing strategy
  • NCAA and institutional compliance guidance
  • Endorsement, licensing, and appearance deal management
  • NIL portfolio tracking and reporting
02

Athlete Management

We provide personalized, hands-on support for student-athletes — helping them stay organized, prepared, and focused throughout their athletic journey. Think of us as your operations team off the field.

From day-to-day coordination to long-term career planning, our management services give athletes and their families the structure and support they need to succeed on and off the field.

  • Day-to-day scheduling and coordination
  • Long-term career and brand planning
  • Family communication and advisory support
  • Deadline tracking for NIL reporting and compliance
  • Relationship management with sponsors and partners
03

Transfer Portal Guidance

Entering the transfer portal is one of the most consequential decisions a student-athlete can make. We help athletes understand the full landscape — evaluating program fit, scholarship implications, eligibility windows, and the NIL environment at prospective schools.

Our guidance gives athletes and their families clarity and confidence at every step, so decisions are made with complete information and no surprises.

  • Transfer portal strategy and timing guidance
  • Program evaluation and culture fit assessment
  • Eligibility and scholarship impact analysis
  • NIL comparison across prospective programs
  • Compliant communication support throughout the process
04

Brand Partnerships

We do the work for you. Our team handles front-end outreach, negotiation, and relationship management across all areas of the marketing and partnership process. We connect athletes with brands that align with their values and amplify their platform.

Every partnership is evaluated not just for its financial upside, but for how it positions the athlete's brand for the long term.

  • Brand outreach and pitch development
  • Sponsorship negotiation and deal structuring
  • Values alignment and brand fit vetting
  • Licensing and merchandise opportunities
  • Long-term partnership relationship management
05

Media Preparation

Today's athletes are more visible than ever. One interview, one post, one moment can define a narrative for years. We help clients prepare for media environments, develop their social media presence, and build a polished, professional public image.

Our media preparation covers communication skills, brand presentation, risk awareness, and on-camera confidence — ensuring athletes represent themselves with consistency and maturity.

  • Interview coaching and press preparation
  • Social media strategy and content planning
  • Personal brand positioning and messaging
  • Crisis communication and risk awareness
  • On-camera and in-person presentation skills
06

Revenue Share Advisory

The House v. NCAA settlement, approved June 2025, opened the door to direct revenue sharing between schools and athletes — up to $20.5 million per school annually. This is new territory for most athletes and their families, and most agencies aren't equipped to advise on it properly.

We are. We help athletes understand their revenue sharing entitlements, evaluate institutional offers in context, and connect with licensed financial professionals to structure and protect those earnings for the long term.

  • House v. NCAA settlement education and context
  • Institutional revenue share offer evaluation
  • Coordination with licensed financial advisors and CPAs
  • Tax planning coordination for revenue share income
  • Long-term financial structure and wealth planning referrals
07

Coach Representation

Coaches face a unique set of challenges when it comes to representation, contract negotiation, and career navigation. Our team understands the nuances of the coaching world and provides strategic counsel tailored to coaches at every level.

We build long-term communication strategies and advocacy frameworks that position coaches for sustainable career success.

  • Contract review and negotiation advisory
  • Career positioning and school evaluation
  • Strategic communication and relationship guidance
  • NIL policy navigation for coaching staff
  • Long-term career planning and transition support
08

Tax Planning

Athletes today earn from more places than ever — NIL deals, collectives, revenue sharing, endorsements, and appearances spanning multiple states and jurisdictions. We bring a forward-thinking, multi-jurisdictional approach to tax strategy built around your full earnings picture. The difference between a tax preparer and a tax strategist is the difference between reacting and planning.

College athletes today earn from NIL deals, collectives, revenue sharing, endorsements, and appearances — often in multiple states simultaneously. We coordinate with licensed CPAs to build a tax strategy that reflects the full complexity of your earnings picture.

  • Jock tax analysis across every state and location you compete in
  • Entity structuring for endorsement and NIL business income
  • Charitable giving strategy with tax optimization
  • IRS and state agency representation coordination
  • Year-round planning, not April-only preparation
  • Multi-state income allocation and reporting guidance
09

Business Management

Behind every serious athletic career is a financial and operational foundation that most athletes never build until it is too late. From entity formation and credit architecture to generational wealth planning and day-to-day administration, we help you run your career with the same discipline and structure as a well-operated business.

We connect athletes with the licensed professionals — CPAs, attorneys, bankers, and insurance advisors — needed to structure earnings properly from day one, so nothing falls through the cracks.

  • Entity setup and corporate structuring (LLCs, S-corps, family trusts)
  • Personal and business credit building strategy
  • Generational wealth planning and estate architecture coordination
  • Cash flow operations and expense management
  • Financial reporting, bookkeeping, and organization
  • Dedicated point of contact coordinating your full professional team
10

Legacy Planning

A great athletic career lasts a decade. A great legacy lasts generations. We help athletes build the structure — foundation and nonprofit planning, family office architecture, and long-horizon financial strategy — that converts years of high earnings into lasting economic power for their families and communities.

Most athletes are so focused on performing that they never build the structure that outlasts the career. We start that conversation early — because the decisions made at 21 determine what's possible at 41.

  • Family foundation and nonprofit formation
  • Estate planning coordination with outside counsel
  • Family office architecture for post-career operations
  • Legacy brand and intellectual property strategy
  • Next-generation financial education planning
  • Long-term investment structure coordination with licensed advisors
